# Tags

<span class="badge text-bg-primary">Beginner</span>

Assets can be tagged to help with organization and assigning them to [asset bundles](asset-bundles.md).

Tags aren't available at runtime. They are only used as a tool in **Game Studio** and during compilation in [asset bundles](asset-bundles.md).

## Add or remove a tag

An asset's tags are displayed at the top of the **Property grid**.

![](media/property-grid-tags.webp)

You can remove a tag by clicking 🗙 and add a new one in the **Add a new tag** box.
